Hey there! Glad you found me. Let's be friends.

I’m a Junior in the user experience design track of the University of Michigan's Bachelor of Science in Information program. I focus on crafting good user experience, writing effective code, and creating streamlined visual design.

Why I Design

I grew up frantically writing down the words that circled through my mind and drawing flowers on the margins of college ruled paper. Inherently logical yet naturally creative, I spent years developing my writing, argumentative, artistic, quantitative, musical, and analytical skills. Newer to the picture is my love for coding, which has worked itself into so many areas of my life, including my current position as a mentor for underrepresented AP Computer Science students. You would be equally likely to find me working with Python or Javascript, creating graphics on Illustrator, writing poetry, or singing with my guitar behind the windows of my favorite coffee shop. My greatest passion is to use these skills to make others' lives a little bit better. UX design is the culmination of everything I am. I did not choose to be a UX Designer. Once I discovered it, my passion for good user experience was so great that I had no choice but to make it a reality.

"UX Design is the culmination of everything I am."


Hobbies and Interests

Outside of work and school, I’m an avid runner (I just completed my third half-marathon!), dog person, music enthusiast (my playlists contain everything from the Smiths to Aretha Franklin), and lover of strong coffee.
